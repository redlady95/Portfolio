About Me

Laura is a Minnesota native, living in Ramsey with her husband Brad and three sons Max(10), Jason(15), and Brandon(19). She also has a 28 year old daughter named Ashley and a seven year old grandson named Kayden. They live Omaha, Nebraska.

Currently Laura works full time at ABRA Auto Body & Glass in Brooklyn Park as a Jr. Database Administrator and part time as a Realtor for Realty Group, Inc in Coon Rapids. And since that isn't enough to keep her busy, Laura is a full time student at the University of Minnesota in the Full Stack Web Developemnt bootcamp.

In her spare time Laura loves to go watch live bands perform and occasionally get up on stage with former band mates. Having performed and toured with 14 bands over the past 20 years, it's fun to rekindle the band life.
